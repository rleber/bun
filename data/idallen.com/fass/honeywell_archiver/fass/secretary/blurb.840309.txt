30/Jan/84 Fass is Here! 
   Yes, FASS is back!  The funniest, craziest, most musical  show   
   on  campus  is  on this week in the Humanities Theatre.  Shows   
   are Wednesday, Thursday, Friday and Saturday; times are  8  pm   
   for all nights but Friday, which has a show at 7 and one at 10   
   (the infamous Friday Late Show).  Tickets are $3 and $3.50 and   
   are available from the Humanities Box Office (extension 2126).   
   Be there or be square!
